Invitation Into Insight

It is a compilation of 83 poems, 81 poems entirely composed and written by King Kidu while two of them he has written with collaboration of other poets; Avatar Josh in the poem titled "Black Panther" and Salim JK in the poem titled "The Invisible Man". The poetic content revolves dearly around philosophy, religion, adventure and romance while the main idea is to celebrate the beauty of poetry.
